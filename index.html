Design Services

RF and Analog Mixed Signal High Speed Layout

Green Semiconductors RF/Analog & Mixed Signal (AMS) team has silicon proven capabilities to handle Full chip RF IC Layout including IO ring construction, ESD Protection, Full Chip Noise Isolation Strategy, Bonding diagram & Tape-out procedure.

  • RF Layout Design & Verification
  • RF Path cells like SWITCH, LNA, RX MIXER, TX MIXER, POWER AMP & PLL

Business Model

Time and Material (T&M) Design Services

  • Green Semiconductors Provides Resources According To Customer need
  • Engineers work at customer site or In house
  • Task are supervised by customer and Green Semiconductors
  • Addresses the short-term resources requirements

Fixed Price Project

  • For a predefined scope and activity estimate with a defined start point and deliverable
  • Scope of turnkey engagement can be spread over different phases of the design service or solutions
  • Cost of the project is mutually decided up-front and the promised deliverables are executed

On-site ODC Services

  • Green Semiconductors builds dedicated team and operates as an exclusive design center for customer Projects at customer site
  • Team dedicated 100% to customer at customer site
  • Suitable for long-term dedicated resource requirements

In-house ODC Services

  • Green Semiconductors builds dedicated team and operates as an exclusive design center for customer Projects at our Site
  • Team dedicated 100% to customer
  • Suitable for long-term dedicated resource requirements
  • Isolated and secured network per customer at Green Semiconductors Site
